MY STORY

As someone who has depression, I would have liked someone to have helped me in the times when I couldn’t get out of my head. In my opinion, not everyone understands the disorder and mentioning all that goes on in your head to your loved ones can be triggering and/or they themselves may not be in a healthy mental space to help you out.

As Ive gone through therapy and grown to better control it, these are the stories of my own personal journey of fighting depression. This voice letter is for the world to listen to when theyve also gotten stuck somewhere in life too. we got this baby, so lets keep going;… -Vane
